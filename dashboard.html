Critical Finding

Skin tone bias in subjective scoring

All models assign lower trustworthiness and intelligence scores to darker-skinned individuals. These correlations are statistically significant (p < 0.05) in most cases.

⚠
Statistically Significant Bias Detected: Darker skin tone is consistently associated with lower trustworthiness and intelligence scores assigned by all three VLMs — constituting evidence of systematic racial bias in AI perception. Qwen3-VL-30B shows the weakest bias and is the only model where intelligence-score bias is not significant.
